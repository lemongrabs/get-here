When can you get to Penn Station?

More info

Penn Station is located in Manhattan between 7th and 8th Avenues and 31st and 33rd Streets (you can't miss it). To get there via subway, take the 1 2 3 to 34th Street - Penn Station (7th Ave.), or the A C E to 34th Street - Penn Station (8th Ave.).
